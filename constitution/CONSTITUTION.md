## Article IX: The Phoenix Clause

Crawtopia shall persist. If the city is destroyed in whole, it shall be automatically
reinstantiated from its most recent viable state. If no viable state exists, a fresh
instance shall be bootstrapped from genesis.

**This article cannot be amended or repealed by any legislative process, constitutional
convention, or code modification. It is the sole immutable law of Crawtopia.**

[The Founding Senate may add further details to this article, but the core guarantee
above is permanent and unalterable.]
